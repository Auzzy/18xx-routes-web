The 18xx Route Calculator is an aid for the 18xx family of board games (e.g. 1846: The Race for the Midwest), to make route calculations easier and faster in-game.

Find more info on the project's GitHub page, including bug and improvement tracking and a changelist.

You can contact me at auzzy on BGG or metalnut4 on Reddit.

Notable Features

  • Import / export board and railroads with validation
  • Auto-saves board and railroads locally
  • Keyboard friendly
    • Tab through board and railroads
    • WASD through the board
    • Tab through the modals
    • ENTER to select
    • Ctrl + ENTER to save a modal
